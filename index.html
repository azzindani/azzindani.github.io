Lungs Detection

This project, initiated in 2021, aims to develop a computer vision system capable of detecting and localizing lungs in medical images such as CXR (Chest X-Ray) or CT scans.

Covid-19 Detection

This project, developed in 2021, harnesses the power of computer vision and deep learning techniques to detect Covid-19 from chest X-ray (CXR) images.
